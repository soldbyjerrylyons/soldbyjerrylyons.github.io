What Counts as Off-Market?

  • Pocket listings sellers aren't ready to publicly advertise
  • Coming-soon properties, days or weeks before they hit Bright MLS
  • Distressed and estate sales found through direct outreach
  • Owner-to-owner introductions for investors targeting a specific block or building

Less competition, less bidding-war pressure, and first look at inventory other buyers and investors won't see until it's too late.
