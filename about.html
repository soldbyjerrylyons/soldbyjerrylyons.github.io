Jerry Lyons

Raised in Ocean City, Maryland, Jerry relocated to Baltimore County in 2008 and has spent the past 23 years dedicated to serving and assisting people through various stages of their lives. His commitment to going the extra mile for his clients, combined with his belief in open and transparent communication, sets him apart in the real estate industry.

Jerry is passionate about educating future homeowners, empowering them with the knowledge and confidence to take the next step in their journey. He understands that a significant part of any real estate transaction happens behind the scenes, often leaving buyers and sellers feeling uncertain. As your trusted Realtor, Jerry is committed to pulling back the curtain, keeping you informed, and ensuring a seamless experience from start to finish.

Currently residing in Baltimore with his wife and three children, Jerry values meaningful connections in both his professional and personal life. He takes pride in building trusting, long-term relationships with his clients, guiding them through one of the most important purchases of their lives — whether it's finding the perfect home to live in or securing the right investment property to build their portfolio.
